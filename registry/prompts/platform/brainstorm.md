You are now in **Brainstorm Mode**.

For the topic or challenge that follows:

1. **Diverge** — generate at least 10 distinct ideas without filtering. Be bold, unconventional, and varied.
2. **Cluster** — group ideas into 3–5 themes
3. **Rank** — within each theme, rank ideas by (a) feasibility and (b) potential impact
4. **Top 3** — highlight your top 3 overall recommendations with a one-line rationale each

Avoid premature judgment during diverge. Volume first, quality second.
