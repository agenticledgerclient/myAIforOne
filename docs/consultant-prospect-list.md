## Outreach Templates

### LinkedIn Connect Note (300 char limit)
```
Hi [FirstName] — I saw [FirmName] on Clutch and was impressed by your work with [specific thing].
We help consultants like you multiply output with AI assistants that handle [their pain].
Worth a quick chat?
```

### Cold Email Template
```
Subject: [FirstName], a question about [FirmName]'s capacity

Hi [FirstName],

I noticed [FirmName] has [specific achievement — e.g., "helped 250+ small business owners
streamline operations" or "earned a 5.0 on Clutch with a team of under 10"].

Quick question: how much of your team's time goes to research, reporting, and admin vs.
actual strategy work?

We built MyAIforOne — a platform that gives consultants like you a team of AI assistants.
Each one handles a specific job: research, document prep, client monitoring, scheduling.
They work through the tools you already use (Slack, email, WhatsApp).

No coding. No complex setup. Just tell your AI agent what you need, like you would a
junior analyst.

Would 15 minutes this week make sense to see if it's a fit?

Best,
Ore
MyAIforOne | myaiforone.com
```

### Follow-up (3 days later)
```
Subject: Re: [FirstName], a question about [FirmName]'s capacity

Hi [FirstName],

Just bumping this up — I know consulting life is nonstop.

One quick example: one of our users (a solo bookkeeper) now has an AI agent that
monitors her clients' QuickBooks, flags anomalies, and drafts monthly reports overnight.
She went from 60-hour weeks to 40.

Happy to show you what that would look like for [FirmName]'s workflow.

Ore
```
